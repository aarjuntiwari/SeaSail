SeaSail

Venture the seas in search of pirate treasure! Defeat other pirates and steal their treasure to buy new, cool skins in the store, or upgrade your pirate and any pets that you earn.

This is a fun, exciting game where 100 players get on their pirate ships and sail in search of treasure. To win, you must be the last pirate standing with the most treasure. Will you emerge as the best pirate?

How to Play

Use the Left and Right Arrow keys to move either... well, you guessed it, left or right! The Down Arrow key helps you slide/dive under obstacles, while the Up Arrow key helps you see at a different angle. Press the Z key to aim, the R key to run, the S key to sprint, the A key to shoot, and the Spacebar key to jump.
